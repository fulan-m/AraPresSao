# AraPresSão

# Land Use/Land Cover Impacts on Precipitation over (Ara)çatuba, (Pres)idente Prudente and (São) José do Rio Preto

</div>

<div align="justify">

This project was developed under a Research Initiation (IC) grant given by the São Paulo Research Foundation (FAPESP) during the period of May 2024 - March 2025. During the period, interesting insights about rainfall patterns at the administrative regions of Araçatuba, Presidente Prudente and São José do Rio Preto, making use of rainfall gauges distributed over the region, mainly from the Water and Electric Energy Department's (_Departamento de Água e Energia Elétrica_ - DAEE, nowadays called SP-Águas) stations. Later use of the National Water Agency (_Agência Nacional de Águas_ - ANA) was made, due to its own indexing of DAEE stations alongside other providers.

</div>

<div align='center'>

## **Results from the projects can be found here:**

### [⛈️ Rainfall trends using CHIRPS 🗺️](https://github.com/fulan-m/AraPresSao/tree/main/I-SICAm-VIII-JOGAAm-UFSCar-2025)

<div align='justify'>

Through the Google Earth Engine platform, the Theil-Sen estimates of rainfall trends over the regions were calculated and presented into different time frames into a poster presentation at the "I Simpósio de Ciências Ambientais VIII Jornada de Gestão e Análise Ambiental" at São Carlos, São Paulo, Brazil.

</div>

### [📉 Rainfall ARIMA modeling ☔️](https://github.com/fulan-m/AraPresSao/tree/main/32-JJI-2025)

<div align='justify'>

Application of ARIMA models over monthly rainfall precipitation measured by rain gauges over the regions and their relationships with Land Use / Land Cover (LULC) changes, presented as a oral presentation at the "32ª Jornada de Jóvenes Investigadores (JJI) at San Miguel de Tucúman, Tucúman, Argentina.
