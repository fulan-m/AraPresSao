# About

<div align='justify'>

Through the use of rain gauge weather stations from the Water and Electric Energy Department (_Departamento de Águas e Energia Elétrica_ - DAEE) of São Paulo state, alongside the application of ARIMA models over the monthly accumulated precipitations measured by the stations, the residues extracted from the rainfall ARIMA models were analyzed alongside the variations of Land Use / Land Cover (LULC) area around 12.5 km buffers around each of the stations.
